## DOERS - Frontline Impact Through Strategic Partnerships

**Objective:** Deliver measurable workforce and health outcomes through coordinated partner networks rather than direct service delivery.

### Key Initiatives:

1. **D1 - Retention Excellence Hubs**
   - Multi-organisational coordination centres addressing the 72% who identify lack of community input as barrier

2. **D2 - Community Map Platform**
   - Real-time workforce intelligence system providing early warning and strategic planning capability

3. **D3 - Cultural Safety Integration**
   - Mandatory ACCHO leadership in all initiatives, addressing First Nations workforce priorities

4. **D4 - Rural Health Innovation Partnerships**
   - Facilitating the 92% who see health partnerships as positive impact

**Success Metrics:** 
- 15% increase in rural health workforce retention
- 25% improvement in workforce planning accuracy
- 100% cultural safety compliance

---

## DRIVERS - Systems Change and Strategic Influence

**Objective:** Lead policy reform and systems coordination addressing root causes of rural health workforce challenges.

### Key Initiatives:

1. **R1 - Rural Health Coalition Leadership**
   - Convening cross-sector partnerships for systems reform

2. **R2 - Evidence-Based Policy Advocacy**
   - Using Community Map data for strategic government engagement

3. **R3 - Multi-Regional Coordination**
   - Facilitating resource sharing and collaborative service delivery

4. **R4 - Innovation Facilitation**
   - Supporting technology adoption and service model innovation

**Success Metrics:**
- 3 major policy reforms influenced
- 5 multi-regional partnerships established
- 20% increase in cross-sector collaboration

---

## ENABLERS - Organisational Transformation and Sustainability

**Objective:** Build internal capability and financial sustainability to support coordination leadership role.

### Key Initiatives:

1. **E1 - Revenue Diversification**
   - Ethical revenue streams aligned with coordination mission

2. **E2 - Data Intelligence Capability**
   - Advanced analytics and predictive modelling expertise

3. **E3 - Partnership Infrastructure**
   - Systems and processes for multi-organisational coordination

4. **E4 - Cultural Transformation**
   - From service delivery to strategic leadership organisational culture

**Success Metrics:**
- 25-30% non-government revenue by 2030
- 90% stakeholder satisfaction with coordination services
