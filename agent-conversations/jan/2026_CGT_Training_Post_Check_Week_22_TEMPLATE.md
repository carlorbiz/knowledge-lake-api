**Future Policy Integration:**

| Has the Practice Manager been informed about the **GP Training Incentive Payments (Semester 1, 2026)**? | ☐ | ☐ | |

**GP Training Incentive Payments Details (Effective Semester 1, 2026):**

For RGTS registrars commencing Semester 1, 2026 and beyond:

1. **Salary Support:** $30,000 per registrar per annum (paid to training post/employer)
2. **Study Leave Support:** 5 paid study leave days per year (NTCER-aligned, in addition to educational release time)
3. **Parental Leave Support:** Up to 20 weeks paid parental leave for primary caregivers

**Accessed via:** PRODA/HPOS (same process as AGPT NCP claims)

| Does the Practice Manager understand the requirements for administering the **5 days Study Leave Incentive**? | ☐ | ☐ | |

**Study Leave Incentive:**
- 5 paid study leave days per year
- In addition to educational release time (16 days per 12 months)
- For examination preparation and self-directed study
- Must be documented in employment contract

| Does the Practice Manager understand the PRODA/HPOS claims process for GP Training Incentive Payments? | ☐ | ☐ | |

**If Practice Manager not familiar:**
- Provide overview of PRODA/HPOS claims process
- Confirm PRODA/HPOS access is functional
- Offer to connect with ACRRM Finance Team for detailed guidance

### 6. NTCER Compliance Check

| Checkpoint | Yes | No | Comments |
|------------|-----|----|----------|
| **Has the practice implemented the monthly billing cycle for registrars (as required by NTCER Version 2025-02)?** | ☐ | ☐ | |

**NTCER Version 2025-02 Key Requirement:**
- **Monthly minimum billing cycle** (effective from pay cycle following 1 July 2025)
- No longer fortnightly or weekly billing cycles

| Is the employment contract compliant with all NTCER Version 2025-02 provisions? | ☐ | ☐ | |

**Key NTCER Provisions:**
- ☐ 12% Superannuation Guarantee (from 1 July 2025, paid with salary from 1 July 2026)
- ☐ Monthly billing cycle
- ☐ Mandated teaching time quarantined in roster
- ☐ Educational release time provided (16 days per 12 months)
- ☐ Leave entitlements compliant

**If not compliant:**
- Provide guidance on NTCER requirements
- Connect Practice Manager with GPSA employment support resources: [https://gpsa.org.au/training-practice-menu/employment-support/](https://gpsa.org.au/training-practice-menu/employment-support/)
