The Parallel Between Prompt Engineering and Questioning Skills
Based on my research, there's a compelling parallel between effective prompt engineering and the art of asking meaningful questions in vocational training settings. Both skills share these core elements:

1. Problem Definition & Clarity
Prompt Engineering: Requires breaking complex problems into precise, actionable queries
Workplace Questioning: Apprentices learn to ask specific questions that reveal underlying principles
Parallel: Both demand clarity of thought and purpose before engaging with the information source
2. Iterative Refinement
Prompt Engineering: AI Engineering Lab research shows it "follows the scientific method—a cycle of hypothesis, testing, and refinement"
Workplace Learning: Mentorship involves asking follow-up questions based on initial responses
Parallel: Both processes improve through feedback loops and continuous refinement
3. Critical Evaluation
Prompt Engineering: Students must assess AI outputs for accuracy, relevance, and bias
Apprenticeship: Learners evaluate mentor responses and ask clarifying questions
Parallel: Both develop analytical thinking and evidence-based judgment
