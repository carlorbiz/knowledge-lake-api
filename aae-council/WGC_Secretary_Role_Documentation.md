## Executive Summary

The role encompasses administrative oversight, membership management, regulatory compliance, stakeholder liaison, and board governance responsibilities.

---

## Core Responsibilities Overview

The Secretary role at WGC is multifaceted, combining traditional secretarial duties with strategic board-level responsibilities and operational oversight. Key areas include:

1. **Membership Administration** - Processing applications, renewals, and database management
2. **Regulatory Compliance** - Maintaining registrations with Consumer Affairs Vic and Liquor & Gaming
3. **Stakeholder Management** - Liaising with council, insurers, suppliers, and sponsors
4. **Board Governance** - Organising meetings, managing papers, and supporting decision-making
5. **Operational Coordination** - Managing bookings, communications, and day-to-day administration
6. **Financial Oversight** - Supporting the Treasurer with invoicing and financial reporting cycles
