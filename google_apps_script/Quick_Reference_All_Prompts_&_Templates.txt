üéØ WHEN TO MODIFY PROMPTS
Shorten Prompts If:
* You need faster turnaround
* Content is straightforward/simple topic
* Target audience requires less depth
* Budget/time constraints are tight
Remove: Extra activities, reduce case studies to 1, fewer quiz questions (minimum 5)
Expand Prompts If:
* Topic is complex or technical
* Compliance requirements are strict
* Audience is highly experienced
* Premium/executive-level course
Add: More case studies, additional resources, scenario variations, supplementary readings
Adapt Prompts For:
* Non-English courses: Add "Generate in [LANGUAGE]" at start
* Certification courses: Add "Align with [CERTIFICATION BODY] standards"
* Micro-learning: Reduce module length requirements, focus on single objective
* Blended learning: Add "Include suggestions for in-person activities"
